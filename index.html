Villo Pharmacy is a Kandy, Sri Lanka based pharmacy and wellness company, focused on providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare solutions to the local community.

WELCOME TO VILLO PHARMACY

Your Neighborhood Health Partner.

Founded in 2025, Villo Pharmacy was born out of a passion to make medicine and wellness products easily available to the people of Kandy. We are committed to offering trustworthy advice, quality pharmaceuticals, and convenient services including home delivery and personal consultations. Our mission is to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and the everyday consumer.
