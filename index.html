WELCOME TO VILLO PHARMACY

Your Trusted Neighborhood Health Partner.

Established in 2025, Villo Pharmacy was created with a clear purpose: to make medicines and wellness products readily accessible to the people of Kandy. We pride ourselves on providing reliable guidance, top-quality pharmaceuticals, and convenient services such as home delivery and personalized consultations. Our mission is to connect healthcare providers directly with the community, ensuring everyone receives the care they deserve.
