Welcome to Villogen Pharma - A Journey of Excellence Since 2010

Welcome to the Villogen story. Founded in 2010 by Mr. Daminda Bandara Udurawana at age 30, Villogen started by importing and distributing pharmaceutical products in the central region. Through efficient management, it has grown into a business conglomerate.

Villogen is the holding company for six subsidiaries: Villogen Pharma Private Limited, A and H Associates, Villogen Property Investment and Micro Credit, British Villo, Villo Auto Cars, and British Vila Holding. In just over a decade, Villogen Pharma now distributes over 30 pharmaceutical and ophthalmic products as sole distributor in Sri Lanka, recognized and trusted by professors, consultants, and medical officers nationwide.

Villogen Pharma — Committed to Excellence, Trusted by Healthcare Professionals

Our Beginning

Villogen Pharma Private Limited was established on March 1st, 2010, when founder chairman Mr. Daminda Bandara Udurawana created a new company for the group's pharmaceutical business. Starting with just three people—Mr. Udurawana as Chairman and Managing Director, Mrs. Varuni Udurawana as Director, Mr. NTB Udurawana as non-Executive Director, and one employee—the company operated from a small rented room in Kandy with one vehicle and a motorcycle.

From Humble Beginnings

Started with 3 people, 1 room, and a vision to transform pharmaceutical distribution in Sri Lanka.

Our Growth

From a first-year turnover of 30 million rupees, Villogen has grown into a strong pharmaceutical market player in over a decade. Our annual turnover now exceeds 100 million rupees. The team expanded from three to around 40 employees, we moved to our own fully air-conditioned office and storage premises holding stock worth over 100 million rupees, and we operate a fleet of 30 vehicles.

Our Objective & Mission

For the nine months ending November 2016, Villogen recorded 130 million rupees in turnover. As a socially responsible company, we improve people's quality of life by providing high-quality, affordable medicines while delivering maximum rewards to stakeholders.

Our mission is to exceed regulatory and quality requirements through motivated, dedicated distributors and employees. Our vision: A healthy life for tomorrow.
